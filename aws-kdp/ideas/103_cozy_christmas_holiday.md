# Cozy Christmas: Holiday Coloring Book

## Why This Idea
Coco Wyo's "Cozy Christmas" was their first Penguin Random House release and sold massively in Q4 2024. Christmas coloring books have a concentrated but enormous demand window (October-December). Our catalog has `4th_july_patriotic` but NO Christmas book. This is a major seasonal gap. Christmas + cozy hygge + bold easy = proven formula. Must be published by September to capture early holiday shoppers.

## Market Analysis
- **Demand**: 5/5 — Christmas is the #1 seasonal event for coloring book sales, gift-giving peak
- **Competition**: 4/5 — Many Christmas coloring books but fewer in bold easy cozy hygge style
- **Timing**: 5/5 — Seasonal but massive; must publish by September for October-December sales
- **Differentiation**: 4.5/5 — Cozy hygge Christmas (not generic Santa/snowman) is a premium positioning
- **Production Fit**: 4.5/5 — Cozy indoor Christmas scenes with bold outlines work perfectly

## Target Audience
Adults and teens buying for themselves or as gifts. Parents buying for family coloring activities. Cozy/hygge enthusiasts who love holiday season. Gift buyers looking for stocking stuffers ($8.99-$9.99 price point).
