# Girl Moments: Cozy Daily Life Coloring Book

## Why This Idea
Coco Wyo's "Girl Moments" is their #5 bestseller — featuring cute girls in daily cozy activities (reading, café, skincare, shopping, cooking). This "lifestyle/self-care" niche is massively underserved in our catalog. It targets the largest coloring book demographic (women 20-45) with aspirational cozy scenes they identify with. High emotional connection = high reviews and repeat purchases.

## Market Analysis
- **Demand**: 5/5 — Women 20-45 are the #1 coloring book buyer demographic, lifestyle content resonates deeply
- **Competition**: 4/5 — Coco Wyo dominates but few others do this well in bold easy style
- **Timing**: 5/5 — Evergreen, appeals year-round with gift potential for birthdays, self-care packages
- **Differentiation**: 4.5/5 — Bold easy kawaii girl lifestyle is a specific aesthetic few competitors nail
- **Production Fit**: 4/5 — Scene-based compositions with single character + cozy environment

## Target Audience
Women and teen girls ages 16-45 who love self-care, cozy aesthetics, café culture, reading, journaling. Strong overlap with cottagecore, hygge, and "that girl" TikTok aesthetic communities.

## Suggested Approach
- **Page count**: 40
- **Style**: Bold easy kawaii — cute girl characters with round faces, simple features, cozy environments
- **Key themes**: Girl reading in bed with cat and tea, café date with latte art, skincare routine at vanity, cooking/baking in cozy kitchen, shopping at flower market, journaling by window on rainy day, yoga morning routine, picnic in garden, movie night with blankets and snacks, bubble bath with candles and plants
- **Title formula**: "Girl Moments: Coloring Book for Adults and Teens, Bold and Easy Cute Cozy Daily Activities for Relaxation"
