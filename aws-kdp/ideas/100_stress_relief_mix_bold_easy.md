# Stress Relief: Bold & Easy Mixed Themes Coloring Book

## Why This Idea
Coco Wyo's "Stress Relief" is their #4 bestseller — a single book mixing many themes (animals, flowers, landscapes, patterns, food) in bold easy style. This "variety pack" format captures the widest keyword net on Amazon and appeals to buyers who don't want to commit to one niche. Currently we have bold easy books in specific niches (dogs, cats, mandala, farm) but NO mixed-theme stress relief compilation. This is the #1 gap vs. Coco Wyo's catalog.

## Market Analysis
- **Demand**: 5/5 — "stress relief coloring book" is one of the highest-volume keywords on Amazon
- **Competition**: 4/5 — Many stress relief books but few combine bold easy + mixed cute themes well
- **Timing**: 5/5 — Evergreen, year-round demand with spikes during holidays and exam seasons
- **Differentiation**: 4.5/5 — Bold easy style + mixed cute themes + keyword-stuffed subtitle (Coco Wyo formula)
- **Production Fit**: 5/5 — Can reuse successful prompt styles from our existing niches

## Target Audience
Adults and teens seeking relaxation. Beginners who want easy designs. Seniors who need bold thick lines. Gift buyers who want a versatile option. Therapists recommending creative activities.

## Suggested Approach
- **Page count**: 50 (slightly more than standard — matches Coco Wyo's 52-page Stress Relief)
- **Style**: Bold easy with thick outlines, simple cute designs, large elements filling the page
- **Key themes**: Mix of 8-10 categories — cute animals (cats, dogs, bunnies), flowers & gardens, cozy interior scenes, food & desserts, butterflies & nature, ocean & beach, patterns & mandalas, seasonal scenes
- **Title formula**: "Stress Relief: Coloring Book for Adults and Teens, Bold and Easy, Simple and Big Designs for Relaxation Featuring Animals, Flowers, Cozy Scenes, Food, Nature And Many More"
