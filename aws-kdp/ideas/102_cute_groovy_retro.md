# Cute & Groovy: Retro Coloring Book

## Why This Idea
Coco Wyo's "Cute & Groovy" taps into the 70s retro revival trend that's huge on TikTok and Instagram. Groovy aesthetic (mushrooms, rainbows, peace signs, VW vans, lava lamps, disco balls) combined with kawaii cute style creates a unique crossover. Retro-cute is trending in fashion, home decor, and stationery — coloring books follow these visual trends. We have ZERO retro-themed books in our catalog.

## Market Analysis
- **Demand**: 4.5/5 — Retro/groovy aesthetic is trending across all visual media and merchandise
- **Competition**: 3.5/5 — Some groovy coloring books exist but few combine retro + kawaii cute style
- **Timing**: 4.5/5 — Trend is growing, not peaking yet; strong for at least 2-3 more years
- **Differentiation**: 4.5/5 — Bold easy + groovy kawaii is a very specific uncrowded intersection
- **Production Fit**: 4/5 — Retro shapes (mushrooms, rainbows, flowers) are naturally bold and simple

## Target Audience
Teens and young adults 16-35 who love retro aesthetics, thrift culture, vinyl records, vintage fashion. Strong overlap with indie/boho/hippie style communities. Great gift for teens and college students.

- **Page count**: 40
- **Style**: Bold easy with groovy 70s aesthetic — rounded bubbly shapes, flower power motifs, cute kawaii characters in retro settings
