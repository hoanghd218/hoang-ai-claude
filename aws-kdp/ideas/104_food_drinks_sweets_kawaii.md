# Food, Drinks & Sweets: Kawaii Coloring Book

## Why This Idea
Coco Wyo publishes food-themed content (avocado toast, pizza, milkshakes, desserts) with kawaii faces and cute styling. Kawaii food is a massive trend on social media — food with cute faces is universally appealing and highly shareable. We have `kawaii_fruit_friends` and `ice_cream_candy_land` for kids but NO dedicated kawaii food book for adults in bold easy style. This fills a gap in our adult catalog.
