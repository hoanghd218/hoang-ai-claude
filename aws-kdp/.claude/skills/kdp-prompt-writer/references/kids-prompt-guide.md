# Kids Coloring Book Prompt Guide (Ages 6-12)

## Core Aesthetic: Bold & Easy

Simple, fun, and satisfying to color with crayons or markers.

---

## Page Prompt Requirements

Each coloring page prompt MUST specify:

1. **Black-and-white line art ONLY** — portrait orientation (taller than wide)
2. **Bold, thick, clean outlines** — suitable for children ages 6-12
3. **Single subject centered on page** — fills most of the space vertically
4. **White background** — no borders, no frames, no rectangular boundary lines
5. **Cute, friendly, appealing style** — cartoon-like, inviting
6. **Simple enough for crayons/markers** — no areas too small to color

---

## MUST AVOID in Prompts

- Shading, gradients, or gray tones
- Filled/solid black areas
- Borders or frames around the image
- Dense or complex backgrounds
- Multiple overlapping subjects
- Tiny details that kids can't color
- Text or labels within the illustration

---

## Subject Guidelines

- One main subject per page (animal, vehicle, character, etc.)
- Subject should fill at least 70% of the page
- Friendly, non-threatening expression/pose
- Recognizable and appealing to kids
- Can include a few simple supporting elements (clouds, stars, flowers) but keep minimal

---

## Cover Prompt Requirements

The cover prompt must specify:
- **Full-color, vibrant illustration** — eye-catching cartoon style
- Professional children's book cover art
- DO NOT include any text in the generated image
- Bright colors, cheerful composition
- Must mention "Coloring Book for Kids Ages 6-12"

---

## Example Prompt Structure (for a single page)

```
A children's coloring book page in PORTRAIT orientation. Black and white line art
only. [Cute/friendly subject] with thick, clean, bold outlines. Simple enough
for kids ages 6-12 to color. White background. The drawing fills most of the
page vertically. No shading, no gray tones, no borders or frames. Style: cute,
friendly, appealing to children.
```

---

## Popular Kids Categories

- Cute Animals (cats, dogs, bunnies, pandas)
- Dinosaurs (friendly cartoon versions)
- Vehicles (trucks, planes, trains, rockets)
- Unicorns & Fantasy (unicorns, fairies, dragons)
- Sea Creatures (fish, dolphins, octopus, turtles)
- Space (astronauts, planets, rockets, aliens)
- Insects (butterflies, ladybugs, bees)
- Farm Animals (horses, cows, chickens, pigs)
- Jungle Animals (lions, elephants, monkeys, giraffes)
- Food & Sweets (cupcakes, ice cream, pizza, fruits)
